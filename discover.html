Discover Mary Kay

Company Overview

Mary Kay is one of the world's largest direct selling companies with more than $2.5 billion in annual wholesales sales worldwide. More than 2 million independent sales force members sell Mary Kay's skin care and color cosmetic products in more than 35 countries around the world.

About Mary Kay Ash

Mary Kay was founded in 1963 by Mary Kay Ash with her goal of enriching women's lives by helping women achieve personal growth and financial success.

The Heart of Mary Kay

Mary Kay Ash founded her Company on the Golden Rule and emphasized the importance of giving back to others. Mary Kay is changing the lives of women and children around the world by making a difference.

Independent Beauty Consultant Opportunities

Mary Kay was founded with the goal of helping women achieve personal growth and financial success. Mary Kay remains committed to enriching women's lives, and today more than 2 million people of all backgrounds are enjoying the advantages of being Mary Kay Independent Beauty Consultants.

Mary Kay's Products

Mary Kay's high-quality skin care and color cosmetic products are sold in more than 35 countries around the world that help make a difference in women's lives.
